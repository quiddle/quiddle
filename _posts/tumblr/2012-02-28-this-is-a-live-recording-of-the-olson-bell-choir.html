This is a live recording of the Olson Bell Choir (which I direct) playing Cathy Moklebust’s Bwana Asifiwe. It’s a great song with difficult syncopation and rhythms. It’s also a perfect illustration of how much the choir has grown over the past several years.

The drums and other percussion were not miked for this recording but they’re still audible- let your imagination fill them in a little bit.
